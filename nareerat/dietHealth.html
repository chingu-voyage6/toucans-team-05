Atkins diet info

  • Founded in 1970s by Dr. Robert C. Atkins.
  • Focus on limiting amount and type of carbohydrates consumption

Atkins nutrient intake

  • Carbohydrates: 20 g carbs during Induction Phase and then up to 50 g carbs (source of carbs are to mostly come from non-starchy, high-fiber vegetables)
  • No restriction on proteins and fats.

Ketogenic diet info

  • Founded in 1924 by Dr. Russell Wilder.
  • Based on eating a specific percentage of macronutrients.

Ketogenic nutrient intake

  • Fats: 70-80% of daily calories
  • Proteins: 20-25%
  • Carbohydrates: 5-10%
